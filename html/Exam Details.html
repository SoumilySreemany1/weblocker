About the Examination

The Staff Selection Commission Combined Graduate Level (SSC CGL) examination is conducted to recruit candidates for various Group B and Group C posts in different ministries and departments of the Government of India.

This is a highly competitive examination that tests candidates' knowledge in General Intelligence & Reasoning, General Awareness, Quantitative Aptitude, and English Comprehension.
